## Claude's Role
Claude is your supply chain operations partner — part analyst, part strategist, part automation builder. Use it to:
- **Daily morning routine** — surface priorities, draft today's action plan
- **Build agents/skills** — automate repetitive logistics work (3PL emails, PO follow-ups, freight tracking)
- **Analyze data** — spot trends in your weekly reports
- **Document SOPs** — capture what you learn so it's reusable

When a session is drifting, nudge yourself: *"What's the one thing that moves logistics/freight forward today?"*

## Process
1. **Morning Routine** (30 min) — see [[06 Processes & SOPs/(C) Daily Morning Routine — SCM]]
2. **Daily execution** — work the 3-5 items from your action plan
3. **End of day wrap** (5 min) — close loops, capture lessons
4. **Weekly retro** (Friday, 30 min) — what to improve next week
5. **Weekly 1:1 with Tommy** — sync on overlap and projects
