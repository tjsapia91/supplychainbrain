# Augusto Starter Vault

> This is your personal Supply Chain Brain. Copy this entire `Augusto Starter` folder to your machine, rename it to `supplychainbrain`, and open it in Obsidian.

---

## Quick start

1. **Read** `06 Processes & SOPs/(C) Augusto Onboarding — Supply Chain Brain.md` first
2. **Open** `CLAUDE.md` — that's the brain's "soul," tells Claude how to work with you
3. **Run** your first morning routine using the template at `15 Meetings & Decisions/Daily Action Plans/_Template.md`

---

## What's in here

- `CLAUDE.md` — your customized brain config
- 15 numbered folders — same structure Tommy uses, so you can collaborate easily
- Pre-loaded SOPs in `06 Processes & SOPs/`:
  - Daily Morning Routine
  - ABC Classification Reference
  - Demand Planning SOP
  - Monday Demand Plan Runcard
  - PO Creation SOP
  - **Augusto Onboarding** — read this FIRST
- Daily Action Plan template in `15 Meetings & Decisions/Daily Action Plans/_Template.md`

---

## What's NOT in here

This is a **starter** — empty of actual data. You'll fill it in as you work:
- Your daily action plans (one per day going forward)
- Vendor profiles (as you meet them)
- 3PL operations notes
- Skills/agents you build
- Project notes

For shared, finished, vetted content — that lives in the **Master SupplyChainBrain on SharePoint** (Tommy will give you the link).

Slack/Teams Tommy or read the onboarding doc again. First two weeks you'll have lots of questions — that's normal.
